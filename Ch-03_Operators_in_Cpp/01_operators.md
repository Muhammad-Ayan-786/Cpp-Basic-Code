# Operator

1. Arithmetic

- *Addition* (**+**)
- *Subtraction* (**-**)
- *Asterisk* (**\***)
- *Division* (**/**)
- *Modulas* (**%**)

---

2. Relational ("Returns either **true** or **false**")

- *Greater than* (**<**)
- *Greater than equal too* (**<=**)
- *Less than* (**>**)
- *Less than equal to* (**>=**)
- *Equal to* (**==**)
- *Not Equal to* (**!=**)

---

3. Logical

- *OR* (**||**)
- *AND* (**&&**)
- *NOT* (**!**)
